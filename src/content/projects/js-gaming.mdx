## Overview

JS Gaming is where I build and maintain Minecraft-focused projects for creators and communities.
The work combines technical implementation, game design decisions, and release management.

## What I Build

- Modpacks with clear progression and stable update paths
- Resource and data packs for gameplay balancing and visual polish
- Internal tooling to simplify iteration, testing, and deployment

## Why It Matters

The goal is a better player experience: fewer setup issues, cleaner progression, and predictable updates.
I treat every release as a product update, not just a file upload.

## Current Focus

Right now the focus is on improving compatibility across pack versions and reducing maintenance overhead for repeat tasks.
