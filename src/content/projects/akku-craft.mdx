## Overview

Akku-Craft is a modular power-bank concept centered on robust hardware behavior and easy expansion.
The project combines electronics, firmware experimentation, and practical field testing.

## Technical Direction

- Modular battery and control building blocks
- BMS-focused safety and monitoring concepts
- Arduino-based prototypes for rapid hardware iteration

## Product Mindset

Reliability and maintainability are core goals.
I optimize for clear diagnostics, replaceable parts, and predictable real-world usage.

## Current Focus

Current work targets better telemetry, cleaner board integration, and improved assembly documentation.
